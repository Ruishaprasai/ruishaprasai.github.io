Sexual Compulsivity Based on Gender

Conducted an analysis to investigate how gender influences libido using the Sexual Compulsivity Scale. Two hypotheses were tested: Hypothesis 1 posited that the overall score is higher for 'other' than for 'women' and 'men', while Hypothesis 2 suggested that individuals in the 'other' gender category would score higher on all questions compared to women, and women would score higher compared to men. You can access the dataset on Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yamqwe/sexual-compulsivity-scale/.
